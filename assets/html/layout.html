MCP OPENAPI

Give your AI chatbot superpowers!

Do you want to transform your chatbot into a truly powerful tool without dealing with complicated integrations or writing a single line of code?

With MCP Openapi, you can finally enhance your AI assistant: direct access to over 400 API services and millions of official, updated, certified data. All with simple natural language prompts — zero coding, just fast and reliable results.

WHAT IS MCP?

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) is the universal standard for connecting AI applications to the outside world. Think of MCP as the “USB-C port” for Artificial Intelligence: a single connection that links any LLM (Large Language Model) to any API, service, or database in a simple and scalable way.

This eliminates compatibility issues, greatly facilitating integration, scalability, and accessibility, and accelerating innovation in the development of complex AI applications.

With MCP, developers, companies, and professionals can overcome technical barriers, optimize project timelines, and embed AI directly into everyday work operations, expanding its possibilities.

HOW MCP OPENAPI WORKS

The MCP Openapi server is open source, free, and ready to use. It is a unique solution that allows AI models to interact in real time with Openapi services: AI applications can connect to the vast library of our marketplace.

With a simple natural language prompt and an AI model like Claude, you can access directly from your PC — and yes, without writing any programming code — over 400 API services and millions of globally certified data.

MCP Openapi is unique because it guarantees reliability, quality, and the breadth of service offerings. All Openapi APIs provide access exclusively to services from market leaders and official primary sources such as the Business Register, Revenue Agency, and Public Registries.

WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH MCP OPENAPI

MCP Openapi is designed for developers, data teams, legal departments, fintech companies, and anyone who wants to extend the capabilities of their AI chatbots. Thanks to MCP Openapi, you can automate thousands of everyday work tasks through simple text or voice requests.

Do you want to know if a company is active, has protests, or who the shareholders are? Want to check a company’s risk score in seconds? Just give a prompt to your AI assistant: MCP Openapi handles it in real time. You can verify companies, individuals, beneficial owners, send SMS and certified emails (REM), validate emails and phone numbers, analyze business risks, access property information, and much more!

HOW MUCH DOES MCP OPENAPI COST?

MCP Openapi has no license or subscription fees! It’s free, open source, open, and built together with the community. You can use its full potential right away.

You pay only for what you use: costs are limited to the API services you actually use through the platform or any fees for external AI models (e.g.,  Claude, Gemini, OpenAI). Maximum transparency, maximum cost control.
